📌 1. Introduction

Welcome to PixelMind AI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you share while using our service. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

📊 2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to operate and improve our service:

  • Prompts you enter: The text prompts you submit are sent to our AI processing server (Cloudflare Workers) to generate images. We do not store these prompts permanently.
  • Usage data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as page visits, browser type, and device type via Google Analytics or similar tools.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for analytics and advertising purposes (see Section 5).
  • No account required: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identification information.

🤖 3. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate AI images based on your text prompts
  • To understand how users interact with our website
  • To serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to collect data about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide relevant advertisements.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy & Terms.

🍪 5. Cookies Policy

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our site.

🔒 6. Data Security

Your prompts are transmitted securely via HTTPS to our Cloudflare Worker. We do not store image prompts or generated images on our servers. All image generation is processed in real-time and discarded after delivery.
