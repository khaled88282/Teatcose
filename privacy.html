HOW WE HANDLE YOUR DATA

Last updated: June 2025 · Applies to pixelmindai.online

1. Introduction

Welcome to PixelMind AI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you share while using our service at pixelmindai.online. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to operate and improve our service:

  • Prompts you enter: Sent to our Cloudflare Worker to generate images. Not stored permanently.
  • Usage data: Anonymous statistics such as page visits and device type via Google Analytics.
  • Cookies: Used for analytics and advertising (see Section 5).
  • No account required: We do not collect names, emails, or personal identification.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate AI images based on your text prompts
  • To understand how users interact with our website
  • To serve relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To improve the performance and features of our service

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to provide relevant ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

You may opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings. For more information, see Google Privacy & Terms.

5. Cookies Policy

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense to show relevant ads

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience.

6. Data Security

Your prompts are transmitted securely via HTTPS to our Cloudflare Worker. We do not store image prompts or generated images on our servers. All image generation is processed in real-time and discarded after delivery.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare Workers AI — Image generation
  • Google AdSense — Advertising
  • GitHub Pages — Website hosting
  • Google Analytics — Anonymous usage statistics
  • Google Fonts — Typography

8. Children's Privacy

PixelMind AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Reach us at our contact page or email historybinr@gmail.com.
